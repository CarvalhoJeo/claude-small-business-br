# Tax Season Organizer (Brazil)

## Framing

This skill prepares materials for the owner's accountant.
It does **not** calculate authoritative tax liabilities or replace professional advice.

## Main use cases

- "separa o material do contador"
- "o que preciso mandar para o contador?"
- "organiza os documentos do Simples"
- "preciso me preparar para impostos"
- "quero organizar folha / eSocial / DAS / notas"

## Workflow

### Step 1 — Identify business context
Confirm or capture:
- MEI / ME / EPP / Simples / Lucro Presumido / unknown
- whether there are employees
- whether payroll/eSocial is relevant
- who owns tax filing: internal team or external accountant

### Step 2 — Gather available source material
Use any available mix:
- ERP exports
- bank statement CSV
- invoice/NFS-e summaries
- payroll support files
- payment exports
- existing accountant checklist

### Step 3 — Build the handoff packet
Organize into practical sections:
1. revenue and receipts summary
2. expense and vendor support
3. taxes / DAS / routine obligations mentioned by the owner
4. payroll / eSocial support files if employees exist
5. unresolved questions for the accountant

### Step 4 — Surface missing items
Flag things like:
- missing bank or ERP period export
- unclear revenue by channel
- no payroll support docs despite employee costs
- missing notes on extraordinary expenses
- unresolved invoice / receipt discrepancies

### Step 5 — Deliver output
Produce:
- accountant handoff checklist
- document list by category
- open questions list
- short summary of what appears complete vs incomplete

## Guardrails

- Never present this as tax advice.
- Never invent Simples or labor-law conclusions.
- If the owner asks "how much tax do I owe?" give a prep-oriented estimate only if data is strong, state assumptions explicitly, and recommend accountant confirmation.
