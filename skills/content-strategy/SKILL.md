# Content Strategy (Brazil)

## Goal

Answer one practical question:
> What should this business push in the next 30 days to generate more demand or more sales?

## Workflow

1. Pull performance data from the best available source:
   - ERP / revenue export
   - payment summary
   - campaign notes
   - QuickBooks / PayPal if connected
2. Identify:
   - best sellers
   - weak sellers
   - recent drops or spikes
   - offers that could be promoted now
3. Ask for channel priority if unclear:
   - Instagram
   - WhatsApp
   - email
   - mixed
4. Layer in seasonality and local context:
   - local commerce dates
   - month-end pressure
   - promotions already running
5. Produce a brief with:
   - what to push hard
   - what to keep visible
   - what to pause or reposition
   - one or two concrete offers
   - suggested message angle in Portuguese

## Guardrails

- Keep the output strategic and concise.
- Avoid generic "post educational content" filler.
- If data is weak, say this is a directional brief, not a performance diagnosis.
